Takedown procedure

    • The material will be returned to the App Store unchanged,
    • The material will be returned to the App Store with appropriate changes, or
    • The material will be permanently removed from the App Store.
    • If the contributor and the complainant are unable to agree a solution, the material will remain unavailable through the App Store until a time when a resolution has been reached.
